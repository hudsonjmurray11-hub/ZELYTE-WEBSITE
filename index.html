No. The pouch is worn in your upper lip and removed after use, similar to a breath strip or mint strip.

Yes — ZELYTE will offer both caffeinated (40mg natural caffeine) and caffeine-free options.

Before, during, or after workouts, competitions, study sessions, commutes, or any time you need convenient hydration support.
