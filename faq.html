Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about ZELYTE — the world's first Active Hydration Pouch.

The Basics

What is ZELYTE?

What is ZELYTE?

ZELYTE is a performance hydration brand that produces electrolyte + caffeine oral pouches. They're worn in your upper lip, where electrolytes and optional caffeine are absorbed through your gums — hands-free, no liquid required.

What category does ZELYTE belong to?

ZELYTE is creating a new category: Active Hydration Pouches. Think of it as how Liquid IV changed hydration powders, or how Celsius changed energy drinks — ZELYTE is creating something new in the performance hydration space.

How to Use

Using ZELYTE

How do I use ZELYTE?

Place a pouch under your upper lip. The pouch will slowly release electrolytes and caffeine, which are absorbed through your gums (buccal absorption) over approximately 20–40 minutes. Remove the pouch when done and dispose of it.

Do I swallow the pouch?

No. Never swallow the pouch. It is worn in your upper lip and removed after use. Swallowing is not necessary and not intended.

How long does a pouch last?

Each ZELYTE pouch releases electrolytes and caffeine over approximately 20–40 minutes, depending on how actively you use it.

When should I use ZELYTE?

Before, during, or after workouts, sporting events, studying, long commutes, travel, or any activity where convenient hydration and focus support is helpful. ZELYTE is designed to be used whenever you need it — without interrupting what you're doing.

Can I use it during sports or a workout?

Yes — that's exactly what ZELYTE is designed for. It supports active hydration during activities where drinking isn't practical, like lifting, playing a sport, or running.

Ingredients

What's in it?

What are the ingredients?

ZELYTE pouches contain electrolytes (sodium, potassium, magnesium), natural caffeine (in caffeinated versions), natural flavors, citric acid, and stevia. No artificial colors, no artificial flavors.

Is caffeine optional?

Yes. ZELYTE will offer both caffeinated (40mg natural caffeine) and caffeine-free electrolyte-only options. The caffeinated version provides clean, focused energy — similar to a half cup of coffee — with no crash.

Is ZELYTE sugar-free?

Yes. ZELYTE pouches are zero sugar and zero calories. They are sweetened with stevia, a natural plant-based sweetener.

Does it have artificial flavors?

No. ZELYTE uses only natural flavors.
